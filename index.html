Don't Post Text as an Image!

Why?

Taking a screenshot of your code, terminal, errors or anything fundamentally text-based is counter-productive for everyone involved.

This:

  1. Removes the ability for the content to be indexed by search engines.
  2. Possibly increases the network traffic required to view the content (Very important for users with constrained internet connections).
  3. Potentially adds an extra click if the person helping you doesn't enable in-line images.
